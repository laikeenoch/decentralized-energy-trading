# ⚡ Decentralized Energy Trading Platform

## Peer-to-Peer Renewable Energy Marketplace with Smart Contracts

The **Decentralized Energy Trading Platform** is a revolutionary blockchain-based ecosystem that enables direct peer-to-peer trading of renewable energy between producers and consumers. Built on the Stacks blockchain with Clarity smart contracts, our platform eliminates intermediaries, reduces costs, and accelerates the transition to sustainable energy by creating transparent, automated, and efficient energy markets.
